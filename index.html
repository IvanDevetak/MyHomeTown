Welcome to my website, I am Ivan, a student of the Business and Technical Faculty of the University of Nova Gorica, based in Vipava. I live in Italy near the border with Slovenia in a village called Števerjan. I am 20 years old and play volleyball at a fairly high level.


I chose my hometown as the theme of the website, as I am very active in the village activities. I am a member of the Mladinski Krožek, which belongs to the F.B. Sedej, which organizes many different events, which this website also talks about.


About the place

Števerjan is a small village in the Italian Brda, located in the province of Gorizia in Italy, close to the border with Slovenia. The village of around 800 inhabitants is situated on a hill and offers a beautiful view of the surrounding hills, fields and vineyards.


The area around Števerjan is famous for its vineyards, being located in one of the most important wine-growing regions in Italy. It produces a variety of wines that are sold throughout Italy and the world. There are also many walking and cycling trails near the village, offering wonderful views of the surrounding countryside.


In addition, Števerjan hosts various cultural and sporting events every year, the most famous of which is the Števerjanski Festival, which offers a rich programme of folk music.
